```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐

│   Imperfect Human Details + Dramatic Chiaroscuro            │

└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## 1.2 KHÁC BIỆT vs ĐỐI THỦ (Visual comparison)

| Element | Đối thủ (AI YouTube channels) | THE QUIET WORLD |
|---------|-------------------------------|-----------------|
| **Skin** | Plastic, mịn hoàn hảo, 1 tone | Subsurface scattering, freckles, birthmarks, uneven skin tone |
| **Eyes** | Tròn, lớn, giống nhau mọi nhân vật | Shape MẮT riêng, iris detail (vân mống), catchlight có vị trí cụ thể |
| **Hair** | Khối lớn, ít chi tiết, "perfect" | Flyaway strands, cowlicks, imperfect parts, reacts to weather |
| **Clothing** | Mới, sạch, generic | Worn fabric, missing buttons, patched spots, wrinkles at joints |
| **Lighting** | Flat hoặc "pretty" đều | ★ CHIAROSCURO — half-shadow, rim light, volumetric rays |
| **Color** | Saturated candy colors | ★ Desaturated earth base + 1 PUNCH accent per scene |
| **Background** | Beautiful but generic | Lived-in, dirty corners, imperfect walls, storytelling details |
| **Overall feel** | "Clean cartoon" | **"Cinematic painting come to life"** |

---

## 1.3 "THE QUIET WORLD LOOK" — 5 RULES

| # | Rule | Mô tả | AI Prompt keyword |
|---|------|--------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Imperfect Beauty** | Nhân vật ĐẸP nhưng KHÔNG HOÀN HẢO — freckles, sẹo, tóc lệch | "imperfect skin, subtle freckles, natural imperfections" |
| 2 | **Chiaroscuro Light** | Ánh sáng luôn có HƯỚNG RÕ — tạo shadow mạnh trên 1 nửa mặt | "dramatic chiaroscuro lighting, strong directional light, deep shadows" |
| 3 | **Desaturated + Accent** | Palette tổng thể bị giảm saturation — chỉ 1 MÀU nổi bật/scene | "muted color palette, desaturated, one accent color pop" |
| 4 | **Tactile Texture** | Mọi bề mặt phải có TEXTURE rõ — gỗ, vải, da, giấy | "tactile textures, visible fabric weave, wood grain, paper texture" |
| 5 | **Atmospheric Depth** | Luôn có particles/haze/dust giữa camera và subject | "atmospheric haze, dust particles in light, volumetric lighting" |

## 2.1 NOAH (Protagonist — 11 tuổi)

### FACE & HEAD

| Feature | Specification | Storytelling reason |
|---------|--------------|---------------------|
| **Face shape** | Slightly angular jaw (not round) — thin | Cho thấy "bé gầy, không được nuôi dưỡng đầy đủ" |
| **Eyes** | Almond-shaped, slightly downturned outer corners | "Sad default" — nhưng khi confident = beautiful |
| **Eye color** | Dark brown with amber flecks near pupil | Warm inside dù bên ngoài cold |
| **Eyebrows** | Slightly thick, natural, never groomed | Real kid — không "styled" |
| **Nose** | Small, slightly upturned tip | Youthful, innocent |
| **Lips** | Thin upper, slightly fuller lower, often pressed together | "Holding words back" body language |
| **Skin** | Light brown, 3-4 freckles on nose bridge, slight dark circles under eyes | ★ KHÁC BIỆT: Dark circles = thiếu ngủ/lo lắng. Freckles = human |
| **Hair** | Dark brown, messy, slightly long, falls over one eye | Hides behind hair = hides from world. Climax: pushes hair back |
| **Scar/Mark** | Small scar on left eyebrow (fell when young) | ★ Detail chỉ chúng ta có — recognition mark |

### BODY & PROPORTIONS

| Feature | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| **Height** | Shorter than average for 11 | "Small in a big world" feeling |
| **Build** | Slim, slight — not athletic | Bookish, indoor kid |
| **Proportions** | 1:5.5 head:body | Semi-realistic, NOT chibi |
| **Hands** | ★ Slightly larger than expected for his age | "Builder hands" — cậu sửa kệ sách, giúp bạn, BUILDS things |
| **Posture default** | Shoulders slightly hunched, head tilted down | "Making himself smaller" |
| **Posture climax** | Shoulders BACK, chin UP, hands visible | TRANSFORMED |

### CLOTHING

| Item | Detail | Storytelling |
|------|--------|------------|
| **Jacket** | Dark navy, slightly oversized, frayed cuffs, 1 button missing, zipper slightly stuck | ★ SIGNATURE ITEM — nhận ra Noah qua jacket |
| **Shirt** | White/cream, clean but slightly yellowed at collar | Mom washes it carefully but it's old |
| **Pants** | Dark grey, hem slightly too short (outgrown) | Growing but can't afford new |
| **Shoes** | Black sneakers, sole separating slightly on left foot | ★ Detail shot potential |
| **Backpack** | Dark green, duct tape on one strap, zipper half-broken | "HOLE in backpack" mentioned in speech |
| **Pockets** | ★ Always has hands IN pockets (Act 1-3) → OUT of pockets (Act 4-5) | CHARACTER ARC visible through body language |

### COLOR PALETTE — NOAH

```
Primary:     #3D3229 (warm dark brown — jacket, hair)
Secondary:   #6B5B4E (muted sienna — skin undertone)  
Accent:      #C8963E (amber gold) — appears ONLY in climax lighting
Avoid:       Bright colors, neon, pure white, pure black
```

## 2.2 MR. CARTER (Villain — 35 tuổi)

### FACE & HEAD

| Feature | Specification | Storytelling reason |
|---------|--------------|---------------------|
| **Face shape** | Sharp jaw, high cheekbones, angular | "Cut" features = cutting personality |
| **Eyes** | Narrow, slightly hooded, pale grey-blue | Cold. Calculating. |
| **Eyebrows** | Thin, groomed, slightly arched | Always looks slightly condescending |
| **Hair** | Dark, slicked back, not a strand out of place | Control. Perfection obsession. |
| **Skin** | Fair, smooth, NO imperfections | ★ CONTRAST with Noah — Carter ERASED his imperfections (metaphor: erased his past) |
| **Lips** | Thin, often in a slight smirk or pressed line | "Above you" expression |
| **Shadow on face** | ★ Always lit with COOL light from above → shadow under eyes, nose | "Looking down" feeling even when at same height |

### BODY & CLOTHING

| Feature | Specification | Storytelling |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| **Height** | Tall — always shot from below when with students | Power imbalance |
| **Build** | Lean, athletic, carries himself with precision | "Everything perfect" facade |
| **Vest** | Dark charcoal, fitted, expensive texture | Money, status, "looks the part" |
| **Shirt** | Crisp white, starched collar, silver cufflinks | ★ Irony: HIS clothes are perfect. He judges Noah's. |
| **Watch** | Visible, expensive | "I made it" — overcompensating for poor past |
| **Shoes** | Polished black oxfords | You can hear him coming (SFX: sharp footsteps) |

### COLOR PALETTE — MR. CARTER

```
Primary:     #2C3E50 (cold dark blue-grey)
Secondary:   #1A1A2E (deep navy-black)
Accent:      #8E9AAF (cold steel) — his lighting always cold
Avoid:       Warm tones, gold, amber (those belong to NOAH's world)
```

# PHẦN 6: AI PROMPT MASTER TEMPLATES

---

## 6.1 MANDATORY PREFIX (Mọi prompt đều bắt đầu bằng)

```
"3D animated, semi-realistic style, elevated stylized proportions (1:5.5 
head-to-body), tactile textures visible (fabric weave, skin pores, wood grain), 
dramatic chiaroscuro lighting with strong directional source, atmospheric 
haze and dust particles in light beams, muted desaturated color palette 
with one accent color, cinematic composition rule of thirds, depth of field, 
contemplative mood, NOT chibi, NOT cartoon proportions, NOT flat lighting, 
NOT over-saturated, NOT candy colors, NOT anime style, NOT looking at camera 
unless specified"
```

## 6.2 MANDATORY SUFFIX (Mọi prompt đều kết thúc bằng)

```
"high quality render, photorealistic lighting on stylized characters, 
subsurface scattering on skin, volumetric atmospheric lighting, 
film grain subtle, 16:9 aspect ratio, cinematic color grading"
```

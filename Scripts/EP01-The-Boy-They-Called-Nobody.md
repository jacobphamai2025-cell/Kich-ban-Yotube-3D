# 🎬 SCRIPT — EPISODE 01 (NEW)

## "His Teacher Called Him Nobody. He Proved Him Wrong."

---

> **Pillar:** 🔥 Injustice & Redemption
> **Structure:** The Hero's Quiet Journey (5 Acts)
> **Duration:** 10 minutes (~1,200 words dialogue + narration)
> **Dialogue/Narrator/Silence:** 65% / 20% / 15%
> **English Level:** A2-B1

---

# CHARACTER SHEET

## Protagonist: NOAH (11 tuổi)

| Mục | Chi tiết |
|-----|----------|
| **WANT** | Được thầy Carter công nhận, được đối xử công bằng |
| **NEED** | Hiểu rằng giá trị không đến từ sự công nhận người khác — mà từ HÀNH ĐỘNG của mình |
| **FLAW** | Quá im lặng — chấp nhận bất công vì nghĩ "mình không xứng đáng lên tiếng" |
| **STRENGTH** | Kiên trì, thông minh, tốt bụng — giúp bạn học bài, sửa kệ sách thư viện |
| **HABIT** | Luôn giấu tay vào túi áo khoác cũ (xấu hổ vì đồng phục rách) |
| **OBJECT** | Cuốn sổ ghi chép nhỏ (ghi lại mọi thứ học được) |
| **COLOR** | Warm brown → Gold (transformation) |
| **ARCHETYPE** | The Silent Fighter |
| **VOICE** | Soft, hesitant at first → Steady, clear at climax |
| **CHANGE** | Từ "im lặng vì xấu hổ" → "lên tiếng vì tự hào" |

## Villain: MR. CARTER (35 tuổi — Thầy giáo mới)

| Mục | Chi tiết |
|-----|----------|
| **Role** | The Wounded Villain |
| **Personality** | Charming với phụ huynh giàu, cold với học sinh nghèo. Classist. |
| **Visual** | Vest chỉnh tề, tóc gọn, luôn đứng gần học sinh giàu |
| **Voice** | Smooth, condescending, uses big words to intimidate |
| **Wound** | (Hinted — không nói thẳng): Chính ông cũng từng nghèo, giờ CHỐI BỎ quá khứ |
| **Quy tắc** | Không 100% evil — có 1 moment nhỏ cho thấy ông BIẾT mình sai (nhìn đi khi Noah speech) |

## Ally: MS. HELEN (62 tuổi — Thủ thư)

| Mục | Chi tiết |
|-----|----------|
| **Role** | The Quiet Mentor |
| **Personality** | Ít nói, observe mọi thứ, cho Noah sách ĐÚNG LÚC cần |
| **Visual** | Kính gọng tròn, cardigan len, luôn có 1 cuốn sách trong tay |
| **Voice** | Slow, warm, deliberate — mỗi câu đều có weight |
| **Key line** | "The loudest voice in the room is not always the strongest. Sometimes... the strongest voice is the one that finally decides to speak." |

## Narrator

| Mục | Chi tiết |
|-----|----------|
| **Tone video này** | Building from quiet anger → triumphant pride |
| **Echo Line** | "Nobody." (Lần 1: insult. Lần 2: reclaimed with pride) |

# SCRIPT — ENGLISH VERSION

## ACT 1: THE ORDINARY WORLD (0:00 — 1:30)

---

### COLD OPEN — Flash Forward (0:00 — 0:12)

**[VISUAL: Close-up of a boy's hands — trembling — gripping the edges of a podium. A microphone in front. Blurred faces of an audience.]**

**[SFX: Microphone hum. Distant murmuring.]**

**NOAH (V.O. — from the future):**
"You called me nobody."

**[BEAT — 2 seconds silence.]**

**NOAH (V.O.):**
"Let me tell you... what nobody did."

**[SFX: Sharp cut to silence. Black screen.]**

---

### WORLD SETUP (0:12 — 0:50)

**[VISUAL: Wide establishing shot. A school building. Grey morning. Light rain. Students rushing inside with umbrellas. One boy — NOAH — walks without an umbrella. His jacket is old, slightly too big. He doesn't run.]**

**[SFX: Rain. School bell ringing. Chatter of students.]**

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
"This is Noah. He was eleven years old. He went to Greenfield Middle School... where he was the smartest kid no one noticed."

**[VISUAL: Noah enters the school. Other kids with bright new bags walk past him. He keeps his hands in his pockets. Head slightly down.]**

---

### CHARACTER INTRO — The Ordinary Unfairness (0:50 — 1:30)

**[VISUAL: Classroom. MR. CARTER stands at the front. Well-dressed, confident smile. He's handing back test papers.]**

**MR. CARTER:**
"Excellent work, Marcus. Ninety-five percent. Your parents must be very proud."

**[VISUAL: He places the paper on MARCUS's desk — a boy in expensive clothes.]**

**MR. CARTER:**
"Sophia, wonderful analysis. Ninety-two."

**[VISUAL: He walks past Noah's desk without stopping. Drops the paper face-down.]**

**MR. CARTER:**
"And... the rest of you. Try harder next time."

**[VISUAL: Noah turns his paper over. Score: 98%. The HIGHEST in class. His face — no surprise. Just acceptance. He's used to this.]**

**[SFX: Chair scraping. Bell ringing.]**

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
"Noah scored the highest. He always did. But in Mr. Carter's class... some scores mattered more than others."

---

## ACT 2: THE CALL + REFUSAL (1:30 — 4:00)

---

### THE CALL — The Incident That Breaks Silence (1:30 — 2:30)

**[VISUAL: Next day. Classroom. Mr. Carter is selecting students for the school's annual Parent Assembly presentation.]**

**MR. CARTER:**
"For this year's presentation, I need students who... represent our school well."

*(He looks around the room. His eyes skip over Noah.)*

**MR. CARTER:**
"Marcus. Sophia. And... Oliver."

**[VISUAL: Noah's hand goes up — slowly, tentatively.]**

**NOAH:**
"Mr. Carter... can I try? I... I've been working on something about—"

**MR. CARTER:**
*(interrupting, not looking at Noah)*
"Noah. This presentation is for parents. Important people will be there."

*(He pauses. Looks at Noah's worn jacket.)*

**MR. CARTER:**
"We need students who... look like they belong on that stage."

**[VISUAL: The class goes quiet. Some kids look uncomfortable. Marcus stares at his desk.]**

**[SFX: Silence. Clock ticking.]**

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
"He didn't say it directly. He didn't have to. Everyone understood."

---

### THE HUMILIATION (2:30 — 3:15)

**[VISUAL: Lunch time. Noah sits alone in the hallway, eating. TWO BOYS pass by — TYLER and JAKE.]**

**TYLER:**
"Hey Noah, heard Carter said you don't 'look like you belong.' That's rough, man."

**JAKE:**
*(laughing)*
"What did you expect? He picks the kids whose parents actually donate money to the school."

**TYLER:**
"Face it, Noah. In this school... you're nobody."

**[VISUAL: Noah doesn't respond. He just looks at his sandwich. Takes a bite. But his jaw is tight. His hand clenches slightly.]**

**[SFX: Footsteps fading. Noah's breathing.]**

**NOAH:**
*(whisper to himself)*
"Nobody."

**[NOTE: ★ ECHO LINE — First appearance. Meaning: accepting the insult, internalizing it.]**

---

### THE REFUSAL — Fear (3:15 — 4:00)

**[VISUAL: After school. Noah walks home in the rain. Passes the school notice board: "ANNUAL PARENT ASSEMBLY — ALL STUDENTS WELCOME TO SUBMIT SPEECHES." He stops. Reads it. Looks away. Keeps walking.]**

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
"The sign said 'All Students Welcome.' But Noah didn't feel welcome anywhere."

**[VISUAL: Home — a small, clean apartment. His MOM is at the table, working two stacks of bills. She looks tired but smiles when Noah enters.]**

**MOM:**
"Hey baby. How was school?"

**NOAH:**
*(pause)*
"...Fine."

**MOM:**
*(noticing his face)*
"Noah? What happened?"

**NOAH:**
"Nothing, Mom. It's... nothing."

**[VISUAL: He goes to his room. Closes the door. Sits on his bed. His notebook is on the desk. He doesn't open it.]**

**[SFX: Rain on the window. Silence inside.]**

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
"He wanted to disappear. He thought if he stayed quiet... maybe it would stop hurting."

**[VISUAL: He looks at his hands. Then at the window — rain running down the glass. His reflection barely visible.]**

---

## ACT 3: TRIALS & ALLIES (4:00 — 6:00)

---

### TRIAL 1 — It Gets Worse (4:00 — 4:40)

**[VISUAL: Next day. Science class. Group project assignment. Mr. Carter assigns groups.]**

**MR. CARTER:**
"Marcus, you're with Sophia and Oliver. Table two."

*(Noah waits. Every group fills up. He's the last one.)*

**MR. CARTER:**
*(dismissively)*
"Oh. Noah. You can... work alone. I'm sure you'll manage."

**[VISUAL: Other students whisper. Noah nods. Opens his notebook. Face blank — but his pen presses hard on the paper.]**

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
"Working alone. Again."

---

### THE ALLY APPEARS — Ms. Helen (4:40 — 5:20)

**[VISUAL: Library. After school. Noah sits at his usual spot — corner table, surrounded by books. MS. HELEN approaches with a cup of tea and a book.]**

**MS. HELEN:**
"You're here late again, Noah."

**NOAH:**
"Just... studying."

**MS. HELEN:**
*(sitting down, placing the book in front of him)*
"I noticed something today. Mr. Carter chose three students for the assembly."

**NOAH:**
*(not looking up)*
"Yeah."

**MS. HELEN:**
"And I noticed he didn't choose the one who scored ninety-eight percent."

**[VISUAL: Noah looks up. First real eye contact.]**

**NOAH:**
"It doesn't matter, Ms. Helen. He'll never pick me."

**MS. HELEN:**
*(pause — long, deliberate)*
"Noah... the sign says ALL students can submit a speech. He doesn't get to pick who speaks. The school does."

**[VISUAL: Noah stares at her.]**

**MS. HELEN:**
"You don't need his permission to use your voice."

*(She pushes the book toward him. Title visible: "GREAT SPEECHES THAT CHANGED THE WORLD.")*

**MS. HELEN:**
"The loudest voice in the room... is not always the strongest. Sometimes... the strongest voice is the one that finally decides to speak."

**[VISUAL: Noah looks at the book. Then at Ms. Helen. Something shifts in his eyes.]**

**[SFX: A page turning. Clock ticking. Rain stopping outside.]**

---

### TRIAL 2 — Writing (Fear + Mini-Win) (5:20 — 6:00)

**[VISUAL: Montage — Noah at home, at his desk. Writing. Crossing out. Writing again. Crumpling paper. Starting over.]**

**[SFX: Pencil scratching. Pages turning. Clock ticking.]**

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
"He wrote. And rewrote. And rewrote again. Every word felt too small. Too big. Too dangerous."

**[VISUAL: Noah reads what he's written. His hands shake. He almost throws it away — then stops. Holds the paper. Looks at it.]**

**NOAH:**
*(reading to himself, quietly)*
"You called me nobody..."

**[VISUAL: He takes a breath. Folds the paper carefully. Puts it in his jacket pocket — over his heart.]**

**[VISUAL: Next morning — he walks into school. Still raining. Still no umbrella. But this time — his hands are NOT in his pockets. He's holding the paper.]**

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
"He submitted his speech that morning. The secretary looked surprised. Then she smiled."

**[VISUAL: Noah slides the paper through the office window. The SECRETARY reads the title. Looks at Noah. Nods.]**

**SECRETARY:**
"You're on the list, Noah. Assembly is Friday."

---

## ACT 4: THE ORDEAL + CONFRONTATION (6:00 — 8:30)

---

### VILLAIN ESCALATION (6:00 — 6:30)

**[VISUAL: Thursday. Mr. Carter finds out Noah submitted a speech. He corners Noah after class.]**

**MR. CARTER:**
"Noah. I heard you signed up for the assembly."

**NOAH:**
"Yes, Mr. Carter."

**MR. CARTER:**
*(leaning in, lowering voice)*
"I'm going to be honest with you. The parents at this school... they expect a certain standard. Are you sure you want to stand up there... looking like..."

*(He gestures vaguely at Noah's jacket.)*

**MR. CARTER:**
"...this?"

**[VISUAL: Noah's face. For a moment — he looks like he might back down. His hand moves toward his pocket — the old habit.]**

**NOAH:**
*(quiet, but steady)*
"I'm sure."

**[SFX: Door closing. Footsteps walking away.]**

---

### DARK MOMENT (6:30 — 7:00)

**[VISUAL: That night. Noah's room. He practices his speech in front of the mirror. His voice cracks. He stumbles on words. He looks at his reflection — old jacket, messy hair.]**

**NOAH:**
*(to his reflection)*
"Who am I kidding? He's right. I don't... I don't look like—"

**[VISUAL: He stops. Stares at the mirror. Almost tears up. Sits on the bed. Holds the paper.]**

**[SFX: Silence. Only the sound of his breathing.]**

**[VISUAL: Then — he sees something. On his desk. The book Ms. Helen gave him. Open to a page. A sticky note in her handwriting: "The world didn't change because someone LOOKED ready. It changed because someone STOOD UP."]**

**[VISUAL: Noah reads it. Holds it. Takes a breath.]**

---

### REALIZATION (7:00 — 7:15)

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
*(soft)*
"He thought about all the times he stayed quiet. All the times he said 'it's fine.' All the books he read alone. All the classmates he helped in secret. All the things he built... that nobody saw."

**[VISUAL: Flash montage — Noah helping a classmate with math. Noah fixing the library shelf. Noah reading alone under a tree.]**

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
"And he realized... he was never 'nobody.' He just hadn't said it out loud yet."

---

### ★ CLIMAX — THE ASSEMBLY SPEECH (7:15 — 8:15)

**[VISUAL: Friday. School assembly hall. Full of parents, teachers, the principal. Mr. Carter sits in the front row looking uncomfortable. Marcus, Sophia, and Oliver have already spoken.]**

**[VISUAL: Noah's name is called. He stands. Walks to the podium. His jacket is the same old one. He doesn't hide his hands. He unfolds his paper.]**

**[SFX: Microphone feedback. Silence. Hundreds of eyes.]**

**[VISUAL: He looks out at the audience. Takes a breath. His hands tremble — then steady.]**

**NOAH:**
*(slow, clear, building)*
"My name is Noah. And my teacher... called me 'nobody.'"

**[VISUAL: Gasps in the audience. Mr. Carter shifts in his seat.]**

**NOAH:**
"He said I don't look like I belong on this stage."

*(pause)*

"Maybe he's right. My jacket is old. My backpack has a hole in it. My mom works two jobs... so I can sit in his classroom."

**[VISUAL: His mom in the audience — hand over her mouth. Eyes glistening.]**

**NOAH:**
*(stronger now)*
"But this year... this 'nobody' read forty-seven books."

*(He looks at Ms. Helen in the back. She nods slightly.)*

"This 'nobody' helped three classmates pass their exams... when they were too afraid to ask the teacher."

*(He looks at the classmates — they look down, ashamed they didn't stand up for him.)*

"This 'nobody' fixed the library shelf... that was 'too broken to repair.'"

**[VISUAL: ★ EMOTIONAL GAZE — Noah looks directly at camera for 2 seconds. Not angry. Not crying. CERTAIN.]**

**NOAH:**
*(the Dignity Speech — quiet, powerful)*
"You can ignore me. You can skip my name. You can call me 'nobody.'"

*(pause — 3 seconds)*

"But you CANNOT... take away what I built. What I learned. What I am."

*(He folds the paper. Steps back from the mic.)*

"My name is Noah. And I am NOT nobody."

**[VISUAL: Silence — 3 seconds. Then — his MOM stands. Clapping. Alone at first. Then Ms. Helen. Then one student. Then another. Then THE WHOLE HALL.]**

**[SFX: Applause building from silence to THUNDEROUS.]**

**[VISUAL: Mr. Carter — sitting still. Not clapping. But his eyes — he KNOWS. He looks away. His hand grips his knee.]**

**[MUSIC: Piano → Strings → Full orchestral swell → Then drops to single piano.]**

---

### FLASH FORWARD PAYOFF (8:15 — 8:30)

**[VISUAL: Same shot from Cold Open — hands on podium. But NOW we see the full picture: Noah standing tall. The audience standing. His mom crying. Ms. Helen smiling.]**

**[NOTE: ★ ECHO LINE callback — "Nobody" — but now it's a BADGE OF HONOR, not an insult.]**

---

## ACT 5: THE RETURN (8:30 — 10:00)

---

### NEW NORMAL (8:30 — 9:00)

**[VISUAL: Monday morning. School. Noah walks in — same old jacket. But different. His hands are NOT in his pockets. He walks with his head up.]**

**[VISUAL: Marcus catches up to him in the hallway.]**

**MARCUS:**
"Noah... that speech. That was... I'm sorry I never said anything before."

**NOAH:**
*(small smile)*
"It's okay, Marcus. You're saying it now."

**[VISUAL: They walk into class together. Mr. Carter is at the front. He sees Noah. For a moment — they make eye contact. Mr. Carter looks away FIRST.]**

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
"Mr. Carter never apologized. Some people don't. But he never called anyone 'nobody' again."

---

### KARMA / CONSEQUENCE (9:00 — 9:15)

**[VISUAL: Principal's office door. Mr. Carter walks in. Through the glass, we see the principal holding a letter — "PARENT COMPLAINT." Multiple copies on the desk.]**

**[SFX: Door closing.]**

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
"Three parents wrote letters that week. By the end of the month... Mr. Carter was transferred to another school."

---

### MOTIF ECHO (9:15 — 9:30)

**[VISUAL: Library. Noah sits at his usual spot. But now — TWO OTHER STUDENTS sit with him. They're studying together. Ms. Helen watches from behind her desk. Smiling.]**

**[VISUAL: The window — sun streaming in. Golden light on Noah's face. He's not looking OUT the window anymore. He's looking at the people AROUND him.]**

---

### NARRATOR REFLECTION (9:30 — 9:45)

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
*(warm, slow)*
"Noah didn't become famous that day. He didn't become rich. He didn't get a new jacket."

*(pause)*

"But he learned something that no test could measure... and no teacher could take away."

*(pause)*

"That his voice... was always there. It was just waiting... for him to use it."

---

### FINAL IMAGE (9:45 — 9:55)

**[VISUAL: Noah's notebook — open to a new page. At the top, in his handwriting: "Things I Am." Below it, a growing list. The camera slowly zooms in. The last word written: "SOMEBODY."]**

**[HOLD — 5 seconds. Golden light on the page.]**

**[SFX: Single piano note. Fading.]**

---

### CTA (9:55 — 10:00)

**NARRATOR (V.O.):**
*(very soft)*
"If this story stayed with you... share it with someone who needs to hear it. Every person has a story worth hearing. See you next time."

**[VISUAL: Fade to black. End screen.]**

---

**[END OF SCRIPT — ENGLISH VERSION]**

## HỒI 1: THẾ GIỚI BÌNH THƯỜNG (0:00 — 1:30)

---

### MỞ LẠNH — Flash Forward (0:00 — 0:12)

**[HÌNH ẢNH: Cận cảnh đôi tay cậu bé — run rẩy — nắm chặt mép bục phát biểu. Micro phía trước. Gương mặt khán giả mờ nhòe.]**

**[SFX: Tiếng micro ù. Tiếng xì xào xa xa.]**

**NOAH (V.O. — từ tương lai):**
"Thầy gọi tôi là 'không ai cả.'"

**[DỪNG — 2 giây im lặng.]**

**NOAH (V.O.):**
"Để tôi kể cho các người nghe... 'không ai cả' đã làm được gì."

**[SFX: Cắt ngang sang im lặng. Màn hình đen.]**

---

### GIỚI THIỆU THẾ GIỚI (0:12 — 0:50)

**[HÌNH ẢNH: Toàn cảnh. Trường học. Sáng xám. Mưa nhẹ. Học sinh chạy vào với ô. Một cậu bé — NOAH — đi bộ không ô. Áo khoác cũ, hơi rộng. Cậu không chạy.]**

**NARRATOR:**
"Đây là Noah. Cậu mười một tuổi. Cậu học ở trường Greenfield... nơi cậu là đứa trẻ thông minh nhất mà không ai chú ý."

---

### GIỚI THIỆU NHÂN VẬT — Sự bất công thường nhật (0:50 — 1:30)

**[HÌNH ẢNH: Lớp học. THẦY CARTER đứng phía trước. Ăn mặc chỉnh tề, nụ cười tự tin. Đang trả bài kiểm tra.]**

**THẦY CARTER:**
"Làm tốt lắm, Marcus. Chín mươi lăm phần trăm. Bố mẹ em chắc tự hào lắm."

**THẦY CARTER:**
"Sophia, phân tích xuất sắc. Chín mươi hai."

**[HÌNH ẢNH: Thầy đi qua bàn Noah không dừng lại. Thả bài úp mặt xuống.]**

**THẦY CARTER:**
"Và... những em còn lại. Cố gắng hơn lần sau."

**[HÌNH ẢNH: Noah lật bài. Điểm: 98%. CAO NHẤT lớp. Mặt cậu — không ngạc nhiên. Chỉ là chấp nhận. Cậu quen rồi.]**

**NARRATOR:**
"Noah được điểm cao nhất. Luôn luôn vậy. Nhưng trong lớp thầy Carter... có những điểm số quan trọng hơn những điểm số khác."

---

## HỒI 2: TIẾNG GỌI + TỪ CHỐI (1:30 — 4:00)

---

### TIẾNG GỌI — Sự kiện phá vỡ sự im lặng (1:30 — 2:30)

**[HÌNH ẢNH: Ngày hôm sau. Lớp học. Thầy Carter đang chọn học sinh cho buổi thuyết trình Hội Phụ huynh hàng năm.]**

**THẦY CARTER:**
"Cho buổi thuyết trình năm nay, thầy cần những học sinh... đại diện tốt cho trường."

*(Nhìn quanh lớp. Mắt bỏ qua Noah.)*

**THẦY CARTER:**
"Marcus. Sophia. Và... Oliver."

**[HÌNH ẢNH: Tay Noah giơ lên — chậm, rụt rè.]**

**NOAH:**
"Thưa thầy Carter... em có thể thử không? Em... em đang viết về—"

**THẦY CARTER:**
*(ngắt lời, không nhìn Noah)*
"Noah. Buổi thuyết trình này dành cho phụ huynh. Sẽ có những người quan trọng ở đó."

*(Dừng. Nhìn áo khoác cũ của Noah.)*
